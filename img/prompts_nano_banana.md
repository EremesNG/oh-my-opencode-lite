# Prompts para Nano Banana 2 - Agentes de oh-my-opencode-lite

Este documento contiene los prompts diseñados para generar las imágenes de los agentes utilizando Nano Banana 2. La temática elegida es una fusión entre **Dioses Egipcios y Cyberpunk/Sci-Fi**, basándonos en las imágenes de inspiración.

Todas las imágenes de los agentes individuales deben tener una relación de aspecto **1:1**, mientras que la imagen de cabecera (header) debe ser **16:9**.

---

## 1. Orchestrator (Orquestador)
**Inspiración:** Osiris (Dios del orden, coordinador, estado).
**Prompt:**
> A highly detailed digital art of a cybernetic Egyptian pharaoh (inspired by Osiris) with green-tinted skin and a majestic headdress. He is crossing his arms holding a high-tech glowing crook and flail. He is surrounded by floating holographic checkmarks, task lists, and binary code streams. The background features glowing hieroglyphs and a dark, mysterious tech environment. At the bottom, the word "ORCHESTRATOR" is written in large, bold, metallic 3D letters with a glowing green aura. Cyberpunk Egyptian aesthetic, 8k resolution, masterpiece. --ar 1:1

## 8. Header Image (Orquestación de Agentes)
**Inspiración:** Estilo de `team.png` pero adaptado a la temática de Dioses Egipcios Cyberpunk.
**Prompt:**
> A highly detailed digital art showing a central, majestic cybernetic Egyptian figure (representing the Orchestrator) with multiple glowing arms, standing in the center of a cosmic, high-tech network. Glowing energy lines connect the central figure to six smaller holographic panels surrounding them. Each panel shows a different cybernetic Egyptian god working on tech tasks: a jackal searching files, an ibis reading docs, a winged goddess balancing code, a goddess designing UI, a crocodile running scripts, and a mummy building architecture. The background is a deep starry space with glowing golden circuit patterns. Cyberpunk Egyptian aesthetic, 8k resolution, masterpiece. --ar 16:9
